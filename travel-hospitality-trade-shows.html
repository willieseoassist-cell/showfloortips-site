Travel and hospitality trade shows connect hotels, airlines, destination marketing organizations, tour operators, and hospitality technology providers in one of the world's most dynamic industries. Events like ITB Berlin, World Travel Market, and the Hospitality Industry Technology Exposition (HITEC) showcase the latest in travel technology, destination experiences, and hotel operations.

The 2026 travel and hospitality trade show landscape reflects the industry's full recovery and transformation, with growing emphasis on sustainable tourism, contactless guest experiences, revenue management AI, and experiential travel offerings. Exhibitors benefit from immersive destination showcases, virtual reality tours, and live hospitality technology demonstrations that transport attendees to properties and experiences worldwide.

Whether you operate hotels, develop booking platforms, offer destination services, or manufacture hospitality equipment, these events connect you with travel buyers, hotel owners, and tourism officials. Explore upcoming travel and hospitality trade shows to plan your exhibition calendar.
