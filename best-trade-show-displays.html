Our Top Picks

Detailed reviews of each display category with links to the best options available.

1

Popup Displays

Popup displays are the workhorse of trade show exhibiting. These collapsible frames expand to create a large, seamless backdrop in minutes. Available in curved and straight configurations, they fold down into a compact carrying case that often doubles as a podium.

  • Magnetic or velcro-attached graphic panels
  • Available in 5ft, 8ft, and 10ft widths
  • Carrying case often converts to a counter
  • Lightweight aluminum frame construction
  • Custom-printed graphics included with most kits

$300 - $1,200

2

Retractable Banner Stands

Retractable banners are the most portable display solution available. The graphic rolls up into a compact base and deploys in seconds. They are ideal as supplemental signage alongside a larger display or as standalone pieces at smaller events.

  • Sets up in under 60 seconds
  • Standard sizes: 33" x 81" and 36" x 92"
  • Premium models include adjustable height
  • Fits in a slim carrying bag for easy transport
  • Replaceable graphic cartridges on higher-end models

$80 - $350

3

Custom Table Covers

A branded table cover instantly transforms a standard folding table into a professional exhibit surface. Full-print table covers with your logo and brand colors are one of the most cost-effective ways to elevate your booth presence at any event.

  • Fits standard 6ft and 8ft tables
  • Wrinkle-resistant polyester fabric
  • Full-color dye sublimation printing
  • Machine washable and reusable
  • Available in fitted, stretch, and throw styles

$50 - $250

4

Modular Exhibit Systems

Modular systems are designed for companies that exhibit frequently and need flexibility. These reconfigurable setups use interchangeable components -- panels, shelving, counters, and lighting -- that can be rearranged to fit different booth sizes from show to show.

  • Reconfigurable for 10x10, 10x20, and larger layouts
  • Integrated shelving and monitor mounts
  • Tool-free assembly with locking connectors
  • Durable construction for years of reuse
  • Interchangeable graphic panels for rebranding

$2,000 - $15,000

5

Fabric Backdrop Displays

Tension fabric displays use a lightweight tubular frame with a fabric graphic that slides over it like a pillowcase. The result is a clean, wrinkle-free display with edge-to-edge graphics. They are increasingly popular for their modern look and easy setup.

  • Seamless, pillowcase-style fabric graphics
  • Wrinkle-free straight from the bag
  • Available in straight and curved profiles
  • Single and double-sided printing options
  • Extremely lightweight -- many under 10 lbs

$200 - $800

6

Portable Counters

Portable trade show counters provide a professional reception area or product demo station that folds flat for transport. Many models include internal shelving for storage and a custom-printed wrap for branding. They pair perfectly with popup displays.

  • Foldable design with internal storage
  • Custom-printed graphic wraps available
  • Hardwood or laminate top surfaces
  • Some models include locking cabinets
  • Ideal height for standing product demos

$150 - $600

7

LED Light Box Displays

LED backlit displays take your booth graphics to the next level with even, edge-to-edge illumination. The backlit fabric graphics create a premium, attention-grabbing look that stands out in dimly lit convention halls. They are the gold standard for high-impact branding.

  • Even, edge-to-edge LED backlighting
  • SEG (silicone edge graphics) for seamless fit
  • Available in freestanding and wall-mounted styles
  • Energy-efficient LED strips last 50,000+ hours
  • Significantly brighter than standard fabric displays

$500 - $3,000

8

Truss Display Systems

Truss systems create an industrial, high-end look using triangular or square aluminum trusses. They are the backbone of large island booths and can support monitors, lighting, and hanging signage. Truss is the go-to choice for exhibitors who want architectural presence.

  • Aluminum construction -- strong yet lightweight
  • Supports monitors, lights, and signage
  • Triangular and box truss profiles available
  • Modular connectors for custom configurations
  • Creates impressive overhead structures

$1,500 - $8,000

9

Portable Literature Stands

Collapsible literature racks keep your brochures, sell sheets, and catalogs organized and accessible to booth visitors. These lightweight stands fold flat for transport and set up in seconds. A simple addition that keeps your booth tidy and professional.

  • Holds standard 8.5" x 11" literature
  • 4 to 8 tiered pockets per stand
  • Folds flat into included carrying bag
  • Acrylic or metal construction options
  • Some models include a custom header graphic

$40 - $200

Trade Show Display Buyer's Guide

Choosing the right display depends on how you exhibit, where you exhibit, and how often. Here are the key factors to consider before making a purchase.

1. Know Your Booth Size

Most trade show booths are 10x10 feet, which accommodates a single popup display or fabric backdrop along the back wall. If you regularly exhibit in 10x20 or island booths, consider a modular system that can scale. Always confirm the dimensions of your reserved space before ordering a display.

2. Consider Setup Time and Labor

If you are setting up by yourself or with a small team, choose displays that assemble without tools. Banner stands and fabric backdrops set up in minutes. Truss and modular systems may require 30 minutes to over an hour and possibly union labor at certain venues.

3. Factor in Shipping and Transport

Shipping costs to convention centers add up fast. Lightweight displays that fit in compact cases can often be checked as airline luggage, saving hundreds of dollars per show. Larger systems may require freight shipping, which should be budgeted at $300 to $1,000+ each way.

  • Banner stands: typically under 15 lbs, airline-checkable
  • Popup displays: 30-60 lbs with case, may require freight for full kits
  • Modular and truss: 100+ lbs, usually requires freight shipping

4. Think About Longevity and Reuse

If you exhibit 3+ times per year, invest in a durable system with replaceable graphics. Replacing fabric prints is far cheaper than buying a new display each time your messaging changes. Modular systems excel here because the hardware lasts for years while graphics can be swapped out seasonally.

5. Pair Your Display with the Right Accessories

A display alone does not make a booth. Budget for lighting, flooring, a counter, and literature stands to create a complete and professional setup. See our booth accessories guide for specific recommendations.
