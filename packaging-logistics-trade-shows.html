Packaging and logistics trade shows serve as critical meeting points for packaging designers, material suppliers, logistics providers, and supply chain technology companies. Events like PACK EXPO, interpack, and LogiMAT showcase innovations across the entire packaging and distribution value chain, from primary packaging to last-mile delivery solutions.

The 2026 packaging and logistics exhibition landscape is driven by sustainable packaging mandates, e-commerce fulfillment optimization, warehouse automation, smart packaging technology, and supply chain visibility platforms. Exhibitors in this sector present working machinery demonstrations, material innovations, and logistics software solutions that help brands and retailers reduce costs while meeting sustainability goals.

From corrugated packaging and flexible films to robotics-driven warehousing and route optimization software, these events connect manufacturers with brand owners, retail logistics teams, and procurement professionals.
