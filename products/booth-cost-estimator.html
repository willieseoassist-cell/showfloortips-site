Who It's For

Trade show exhibitors consistently underestimate booth costs because they focus on the obvious expenses like space rental and overlook the dozens of smaller line items that add up fast. Drayage alone can add thousands of dollars to your bill if you are not prepared. This estimator makes sure nothing surprises you.

If you are preparing a trade show budget for executive approval, this tool gives you a defensible, itemized estimate instead of a rough guess. The reference price columns help you benchmark vendor quotes against industry norms so you know whether you are getting a fair deal.

It is especially valuable for exhibitors new to a venue or show. Convention center costs vary significantly from city to city, and the estimator prompts you to research and account for location-specific expenses that experienced exhibitors learn about the hard way.

Frequently Asked Questions

What costs does the Booth Cost Estimator cover?

The estimator covers booth space rental, design and construction, graphics and signage, furniture rental, flooring, lighting, electrical and internet, shipping and drayage, installation and dismantle labor, AV equipment, and commonly overlooked hidden costs like supervisor fees and material handling surcharges.

How is this different from the Budget & ROI Calculator?

The Booth Cost Estimator focuses specifically on booth-related expenses with granular line items for construction, materials, and show services. The Budget and ROI Calculator is broader, covering your entire trade show investment including travel, marketing, and staffing, plus ROI calculation. Many exhibitors use both together.

Does it include typical pricing benchmarks?

Yes. The spreadsheet includes reference columns with typical price ranges for each line item based on industry data. This helps you evaluate vendor quotes and identify areas where you may be overpaying.

Can I use this for multiple booth sizes?

Yes. The estimator includes tabs for different booth configurations including 10x10, 10x20, 20x20, and custom island booths. Each tab has pre-populated line items appropriate for that booth size.
