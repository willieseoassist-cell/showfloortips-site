Widget 1

Upcoming Trade Shows

A compact card listing the next upcoming trade shows, filterable by industry. Perfect for sidebars and resource pages.

Embed Code 
<!-- Upcoming Trade Shows Widget -->
<div id="sft-widget"
     data-category="technology"
     data-count="5"
     data-theme="light"></div>
<script src="https://showfloortips.com/embed/widget.js"></script>

Configuration Options

AttributeValuesDefault
data-category all, technology, healthcare, manufacturing, food-beverage, automotive, energy, etc. all
data-count 1 to 20 5
data-theme light, dark light

Trade Show Calendar
