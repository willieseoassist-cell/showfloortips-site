Our Mission

ShowFloorTips exists to make trade show exhibiting smarter, more profitable, and less stressful. We believe every exhibitor — from a first-timer with a 10x10 booth to a Fortune 500 company with an island exhibit — deserves access to the same quality intelligence.

The trade show industry generates over $100 billion annually, yet most exhibitors still plan their shows using scattered spreadsheets, outdated directories, and word of mouth. We're changing that.

What We Offer

ShowFloorTips is the world's most comprehensive trade show intelligence platform:

  • 24,600+ trade shows across 120+ countries with venue details, booth costs, and exhibitor tips
  • 24,000+ articles — networking guides, cost breakdowns, comparisons, and industry news
  • Free tools — ROI calculator, show comparison, packing list generator, trip cost estimator
  • Travel guides — city-by-city hotel, restaurant, and transport recommendations
  • Digital products — checklists, spreadsheets, email templates, and strategy guides
  • Scannly — our AI-powered lead capture app for badge scanning

Our Values

Data First

Every recommendation is backed by real numbers — booth costs, attendance figures, and ROI calculations.

Exhibitor Focused

We write for the people standing in the booth, not the people running the show. Our advice is practical and actionable.

Comprehensive

If a trade show exists, we want it in our database. If an exhibitor has a question, we want the answer on our site.

Free Access

The database, tools, articles, and guides are free. Premium products exist for exhibitors who want to go deeper.
