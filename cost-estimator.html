Smart Budget Tips for Exhibitors

Proven strategies to reduce your trade show costs without cutting impact

Book Hotels Early

Convention-block hotel rates sell out fast. Book 3-6 months ahead and check for rates on the show's official housing page before going to third-party sites.

Share Booth Costs

Partnering with a complementary company on a larger booth can reduce per-company costs by 30-40% while doubling your foot traffic and lead pool.

Ship Smart

Use the show's official drayage vendor for heavy items but ship lightweight materials via FedEx/UPS directly to the hotel. Saves up to 60% on logistics.

Right-Size Your Team

One staffer per 50 sq ft of booth space is the industry standard. Sending extra people inflates travel costs without improving lead capture rate.

Fly on Off-Peak Days

Arriving a day before setup and departing the morning after the show ends can save 25-40% on airfare versus flying on peak travel days.

Track ROI Religiously

Use our ROI Calculator after each show. Companies that track ROI spend 23% less per qualified lead over time by cutting underperforming shows.
