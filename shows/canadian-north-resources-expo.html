Pre-Show Preparation

Success at Canadian North Resources Expo starts weeks before the show opens:

  • Set clear goals: Define what success looks like for your team - leads generated, meetings booked, partnerships formed, or brand awareness metrics.
  • Book early: Premium booth locations go fast. Register early for the best positioning and visibility on the show floor.
  • Promote your presence: Email your customers, post on social media, and update your website at least 4-6 weeks before the show.
  • Train your team: Everyone at your booth should know your key messages, lead qualification criteria, and demo flow.
  • Prepare collateral: Order business cards, brochures, and promotional items well in advance. Consider digital alternatives.

Lead Capture Strategy

Post-Show Follow-Up

The real work begins after Canadian North Resources Expo ends. Follow up within 48 hours, personalize every outreach referencing specific conversations, and add all leads to your nurture pipeline. Track conversion rates to calculate your true ROI.
