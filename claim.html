Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify I'm the organizer?

After submitting your claim, we verify ownership through email domain matching (e.g., you@yourshow.com), a verification link on your official website, or a quick phone call. Most claims are verified within 24-48 hours.

Can I try Pro before committing?

Yes. Start with our free Starter plan to claim your listing and see your basic analytics. When you're ready to upgrade, Pro features activate instantly. We also offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans.

What analytics do I get?

Starter includes monthly page views. Pro adds detailed analytics including views, saves, clicks, referral sources, and a monthly performance report email. Premium adds competitor benchmarking so you can see how your listing compares to similar shows.

How does featured placement work?

Pro listings are prioritized in category and city search results, and appear first in Related Shows sidebars on competing show pages. Premium listings also rotate on the homepage spotlight, visible to every visitor, and are featured in our weekly newsletter.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. All paid plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment required. Cancel anytime and your listing reverts to the free Starter tier. Annual Premium contracts lock in a discounted rate of $399/month (saving $1,200/year).
