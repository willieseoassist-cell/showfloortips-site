Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add or update a trade show listing?
Email us at info@showfloortips.com with the show name, dates, venue, and any relevant details. We add new shows regularly and aim to respond within 48 hours.
Is ShowFloorTips free to use?
Yes. The entire database, all 24,000+ articles, and all tools (ROI calculator, show comparison, packing list, etc.) are completely free. We also sell premium digital products for exhibitors who want deeper resources.
How do I get a refund on a digital product?
Email info@showfloortips.com within 30 days of purchase. Digital products are delivered instantly, but we offer refunds if the product doesn't meet your expectations.
Can I write for ShowFloorTips?
We welcome guest contributions from trade show industry professionals. Email us with your topic idea, a brief outline, and your background. Published contributors receive a byline and link back to their company.
How often is the database updated?
We add new shows and update existing listings multiple times per week. News articles are published daily. If you spot outdated information, email us and we'll correct it within 24 hours.
