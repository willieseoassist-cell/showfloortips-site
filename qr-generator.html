Using QR Codes at Trade Shows

Practical tips to get the most value from QR codes on the show floor.

Place Codes at Eye Level

Mount QR codes between 4 and 5 feet high on your booth walls or banner stands. Codes placed too low or too high are easily missed by passing attendees.

Test Before You Print

Always scan your QR code with multiple devices before sending it to the printer. Test on both iOS and Android phones under various lighting conditions.

Use a Clear Call to Action

Never post a bare QR code. Add a short instruction like "Scan for our product catalog" or "Scan to share your contact info" so attendees know what to expect.

Size Matters for Scanning Distance

A QR code should be at least 1 inch for every 10 inches of scanning distance. For a banner viewed from 6 feet away, the code should be at least 7 inches wide.

Link to Mobile-Optimized Content

Attendees will scan with their phones. Make sure the destination page loads fast, is mobile-friendly, and does not require a login or file download.

Keep High Contrast

Dark foreground on a light background scans most reliably. Avoid low-contrast color combinations or placing codes on busy, patterned backgrounds.
