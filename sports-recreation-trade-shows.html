Sports and recreation trade shows bring together athletic brands, fitness equipment manufacturers, outdoor recreation companies, and sports facility operators to showcase the latest products and innovations. Events like the Outdoor Retailer Show, IHRSA, and the Sports Licensing & Tailgate Show serve diverse segments of the active lifestyle industry.

In 2026, sports and recreation trade shows reflect trends toward wearable fitness technology, sustainable athletic gear, adventure tourism, e-sports crossover, and inclusive recreation programs. Exhibitors in this space thrive with hands-on product testing areas, athlete endorsement activations, and performance demonstration zones that let buyers experience equipment and apparel firsthand.

From team sports and fitness technology to outdoor adventure and recreation facility management, these events connect brands with retailers, facility operators, and purchasing managers across the sports industry. Browse upcoming events to find the right shows for your product lines.
