Pet and animal trade shows connect pet product manufacturers, veterinary suppliers, animal nutrition companies, and pet retail chains in one of the fastest-growing consumer industries worldwide. Events like Global Pet Expo, SuperZoo, and Interzoo bring together thousands of brands showcasing pet food, accessories, veterinary equipment, and pet care innovations.

In 2026, the pet industry trade show circuit reflects the ongoing humanization of pets trend, with growing emphasis on premium nutrition, pet health technology, sustainable pet products, and direct-to-consumer pet brands. Exhibitors benefit from product sampling opportunities, buyer meetings, and new product launch platforms that generate significant retailer interest and media coverage.

Whether you manufacture pet food, develop pet health technologies, produce grooming products, or supply veterinary equipment, pet and animal trade shows connect you with major pet retailers, veterinary distributors, and e-commerce buyers.
