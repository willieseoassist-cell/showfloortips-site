Defense and security trade shows are critical forums where military organizations, law enforcement agencies, homeland security departments, and defense contractors come together to evaluate weapons systems, surveillance technologies, cybersecurity solutions, and protective equipment. Events like AUSA, DSEI, and IDEX are among the largest and most strategically important in the global defense industry.

In 2026, the defense exhibition circuit reflects heightened global security concerns, with growing emphasis on autonomous systems, electronic warfare, space defense, cyber resilience, and multi-domain operations. Exhibitors in this sector must navigate ITAR regulations, security clearance requirements, and government procurement processes while delivering compelling demonstrations of mission-critical capabilities.

For defense contractors, security technology firms, and dual-use innovators, these events provide direct access to military procurement officials, allied nation delegations, and government program managers. Browse the directory below to find upcoming defense and security exhibitions worldwide.
