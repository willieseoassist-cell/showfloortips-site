Teardown and Dismantling Checklist for Beer Warsaw Expo - Trade Fair for Brewing Products

ShowFloorTips · January 23, 2026

Smart exhibitors treat trade shows as a complete marketing campaign, not a one-off event. Teardown at Beer Warsaw Expo - Trade Fair for Brewing Products requires the same level of organization as setup. This checklist ensures efficient dismantling of your booth at in Warsaw while protecting your materials and capturing final opportunities.

Before the Show Closes

  • Confirm your shipping arrangements and return freight pickup time with your carrier.
  • Begin pre-packing non-essential items during the final hours if show rules allow.
  • Back up all lead data to the cloud and export a copy to your CRM or email.
  • Collect any remaining materials from meeting rooms, press areas, or information desks.
  • Take final photos of the booth for your post-show report and future planning.
  • Thank neighboring exhibitors and exchange contact information for future collaboration.

Dismantling Process

  • Wait for the official teardown announcement before beginning disassembly.
  • Disconnect and pack all technology first: devices, cables, adapters, and chargers into clearly labeled cases.
  • Remove graphics, signage, and branding elements carefully to preserve them for future shows.
  • Disassemble the booth structure in reverse order of assembly, following your setup guide.
  • Inspect all components for damage and note anything that needs repair or replacement before the next event.
  • Pack materials securely using original packaging where available, and label all crates and cases clearly.
4hrs
typical teardown time for a standard booth

Freight and Shipping

  • Complete all shipping labels and Bill of Lading documents for return freight.
  • Deliver packed materials to the designated freight staging area within the venue's required timeframe.
  • Obtain receipts for all shipped items and take photos of labeled crates for tracking purposes.
  • Confirm estimated delivery date at your warehouse or office and schedule receiving.
  • Return all rented equipment (furniture, AV, lead retrieval devices) to the designated return location.

Final Venue Checkout

  • Clean your booth space and remove all materials, including tape residue and floor coverings.
  • Complete the exhibitor checkout process with show management.
  • Return exhibitor badges if required by the venue.
  • Settle any outstanding invoices with the venue or show organizer for services rendered.
  • Conduct a final walk-through of the area to ensure nothing is left behind.
