Pre-Show Planning Checklist for Paysalia - Lanscape, Garden & Sport Exhibition

ShowFloorTips · January 14, 2026

The exhibitors who dominate the floor at Paysalia - Lanscape, Garden & Sport Exhibition do not wing it. They arrive with a plan that was finalized weeks ago. This pre-show planning checklist ensures nothing falls through the cracks.

8 Weeks Before Paysalia - Lanscape, Garden & Sport Exhibition

  • Confirm booth space and review floor plan for optimal positioning
  • Finalize booth design concept and begin production
  • Book team travel and accommodations in Lyon
  • Set measurable objectives: target number of leads, meetings, and deals
  • Order promotional materials, giveaways, and printed collateral

4 Weeks Before

  • Launch pre-show email campaign to prospects and existing customers
  • Schedule meetings with target accounts attending the event
  • Confirm shipping logistics for booth materials and displays
  • Prepare product demonstrations and talking points
  • Set up digital lead capture tool (Scannly recommended)

1 Week Before

  • Conduct team briefing: roles, schedules, dress code, talking points
  • Confirm all shipments have arrived at the venue
  • Test all technology: tablets, monitors, Wi-Fi hotspots, payment systems
  • Send final reminder to scheduled meetings
  • Pack emergency kit: extension cords, tape, markers, pain relievers, phone chargers
Pre-Show RuleIf you finish your preparation and feel like you have nothing left to do, you are ready. If you are still scrambling the night before setup, you started too late. Aim to be completely prepared seven days before the show opens.
