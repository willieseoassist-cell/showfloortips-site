Computex 2026 Exhibitor FAQ: Everything You Need to Know

ShowFloorTips Editorial February 11, 2026

Computex Taipei is Asia's largest and the world's second-largest computer and technology trade show, attracting 40,000+ attendees and 1,600+ exhibitors to Taipei Nangang Exhibition Center in Taipei, Taiwan. Whether you are a first-time exhibitor or a returning company, this FAQ covers the most common questions about exhibiting at Computex Taipei.

How much does booth space cost?

Booth space costs approximately $350-500 per 9 sqm booth unit per square foot. A 10x10 booth runs Approximately $3,500 - $5,000 per 9 sqm booth (one unit), a 10x20 costs Approximately $7,000 - $10,000 for 2 booth units (18 sqm), and a 20x20 costs Approximately $14,000 - $20,000 for 4 booth units (36 sqm). Computex uses 3m x 3m (9 sqm) booth units. All booths must be ordered in multiples of 9 sqm. A non-refundable deposit of US$1,000 per booth unit is required upon application acceptance. All booths receive 110V/500W electricity free of charge.

When and where is the show?

Computex Taipei takes place June 2-6, 2026 at Taipei Nangang Exhibition Center in Taipei, Taiwan. The show attracts 40,000+ attendees and 1,600+ exhibitors, making it one of the most important events in its industry.

What is the exhibitor registration deadline?

Computex opens overseas exhibitor applications approximately 8-10 months before the show. A signed application and $1,000/booth deposit secure your spot. Full payment due within 2 weeks of booth allocation. Contact my.computex.biz for applications.

How do I ship my booth materials and equipment?

International shipping to Taiwan requires customs clearance. Use a freight forwarder experienced with Taiwan trade shows. ATA Carnets accepted. Allow 4-8 weeks for sea freight or 1-2 weeks for air freight. TAITRA provides logistics support through the exhibitor portal.

What hotels are recommended near the venue?

Taipei hotels near Nangang range from TWD 3,000-8,000/night ($95-$250). The Grand Hyatt Taipei, Taipei Marriott, and numerous business hotels are nearby. Taipei's MRT system makes hotels throughout the city convenient.

How many exhibitor badges are included with my booth?

Exhibitor badge allocation based on booth size. Additional badges can be purchased. Online pre-registration required for all staff.

What are the setup and teardown schedules?

Move-in typically begins 2-3 days before the show. Shell scheme booths include basic walls, carpet, and signage. Raw space builds must comply with Nangang Exhibition Center safety regulations.

What lead retrieval options are available?

Computex offers digital lead retrieval through badge scanning. International exhibitors should bring their own scanning solutions for reliability. Scannly works with any badge format. For a more advanced solution, Scannly provides AI-powered badge scanning with automated lead qualification and follow-up capabilities.

Is there parking at the venue?

Nangang Exhibition Center has underground parking. Daily rates approximately TWD 200-400 ($6-$12). The Nangang MRT/TRA station is directly connected to the venue.

What types of companies exhibit at this show?

Exhibitors include computer hardware, AI and high-performance computing, gaming peripherals, networking equipment, storage solutions, display technology, semiconductors, IoT devices, and smart solutions. Major brands include NVIDIA, AMD, Intel, ASUS, MSI, and thousands of component manufacturers.
