Exhibitor's Guide to KINTEX

ShowFloorTips · February 07, 2026

Trade shows remain one of the most powerful channels for B2B growth, and the data backs it up. KINTEX in Goyang, South Korea is one of the world's leading exhibition venues, and understanding its layout, services, and logistics is essential for any exhibitor planning to showcase there. This guide covers everything you need for a smooth and successful exhibition experience at KINTEX.

Location and Access

KINTEX is located in Goyang, South Korea. The venue is accessible via Incheon International Airport (40 min). Whether you are traveling domestically or internationally, the transportation infrastructure makes reaching KINTEX straightforward for exhibitors and attendees alike.

For exhibitors shipping booth materials, the venue provides dedicated freight entrances and loading docks with advance warehouse receiving options. Contact the venue's exhibitor services team at least six weeks before your event to coordinate delivery schedules and avoid congestion during peak setup periods.

Venue Layout and Facilities

KINTEX features 108,000 sq m of exhibit space, the largest venue in South Korea. The venue is designed to accommodate events of varying scales, from intimate conferences to massive international trade shows with thousands of exhibitors.

Key features of the venue include climate-controlled exhibition halls, on-site business centers, VIP lounges, food courts and catering services, press rooms, and modern telecommunications infrastructure including high-speed Wi-Fi throughout. Exhibitors benefit from well-planned aisle layouts that maximize foot traffic to all booth locations.

When selecting your booth position, request a detailed floor plan from the show organizer and prioritize locations near main entrances, registration areas, food service zones, or keynote stages. These high-traffic positions consistently deliver more visitor engagement, though they often come at a premium price.

Exhibitor Services

KINTEX offers comprehensive exhibitor services designed to simplify your trade show experience. These typically include:

  • Electrical and utility connections with various amperage and voltage options to power your booth equipment and lighting.
  • Internet and telecommunications packages including dedicated bandwidth for streaming, demonstrations, and lead capture systems.
  • Furniture and equipment rentals ranging from basic tables and chairs to premium display cases and AV equipment.
  • On-site security services including after-hours booth monitoring to protect your valuable display materials and products.
  • Cleaning and waste removal services to keep your booth presentable throughout multi-day events.
  • Rigging and hanging point services for overhead signage, banners, and suspended displays.
  • On-site printing and signage services for last-minute needs and replacements.

Order services through the venue's exhibitor services portal well in advance to secure the best rates. Last-minute orders typically incur surcharges of 25-50% above standard pricing.

Nearby Hotels and Accommodations

Recommended hotels near KINTEX include KINTEX MVL Hotel, Lotte City Hotel. During major trade shows, hotel inventory in Goyang fills up quickly, so book accommodations as soon as your exhibition participation is confirmed.

Most shows hosted at KINTEX offer official hotel blocks with negotiated group rates and shuttle service between the hotel and venue. These rates typically represent 15-25% savings compared to standard booking, and the convenience of shuttle service eliminates the need for rental cars or ride-sharing during the event.

For teams of four or more, consider serviced apartments or suite-style hotels that provide meeting space and shared living areas, which can reduce per-person costs while offering a more comfortable extended-stay experience.

Transportation and Getting Around

Reaching KINTEX from the airport is straightforward via Incheon International Airport (40 min). Public transportation, taxis, and ride-sharing services all provide convenient connections between the airport, hotels, and the venue.

For exhibitors with large booth materials, coordinate freight transportation separately from your personal travel. The venue's freight office can provide recommended carriers who are familiar with the facility's loading dock procedures and delivery windows.

During setup and teardown days, allow extra travel time as traffic around KINTEX increases significantly. Many experienced exhibitors schedule arrivals the day before setup begins to avoid the rush and ensure a stress-free start to the event.

Setup and Logistics Tips for KINTEX

A successful exhibition at KINTEX starts with meticulous planning. These tips from experienced exhibitors will help you navigate the setup process:

  • Confirm all venue regulations regarding booth height, electrical requirements, and fire safety before designing your space.
  • Pack a separate essentials kit with tools, tape, zip ties, markers, and first-aid supplies for last-minute adjustments.
  • Plan for dismantling and return shipping before the show begins to avoid scrambling during teardown.
  • Create a comprehensive packing list that includes every item needed for setup, from extension cords to business cards.

Booth Design Considerations

Designing your booth for KINTEX requires understanding the venue's specific characteristics, including ceiling heights, aisle widths, utility access points, and lighting conditions. Consider these design strategies:

  • Select a color palette that aligns with your brand but contrasts with the typical show floor environment to stand out.
  • Use bold graphics at eye level and above to capture attention from across the hall. Your messaging should be readable from at least 15 feet away.
  • Use modular displays that can be reconfigured for different show sizes and booth dimensions.
  • Invest in a clean, open booth layout that invites attendees inside rather than blocking them with tables or barriers.

Final Tips for Exhibiting at KINTEX

KINTEX is a world-class venue that rewards well-prepared exhibitors with strong audience engagement and lead generation opportunities. Invest time in understanding the venue's layout and services, book accommodations and logistics early, and take advantage of the exhibitor support resources available.
