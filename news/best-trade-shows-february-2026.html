Tips for February Trade Shows

Preparing for a February event requires attention to seasonal factors, booking timelines, and industry cycles. Here are key considerations for exhibitors:

  • Lighting is often underestimated. Use spotlights and accent lighting to draw attention to key products and create visual hierarchy.
  • Invest in a clean, open booth layout that invites attendees inside rather than blocking them with tables or barriers.
  • Consider vertical space. Hanging signs and tall structures increase visibility in crowded venues.

"Trade show booth visitors spend an average of 5-15 minutes at each exhibit they visit."

-- Frank DeLuca, CEO, Exhibitor Magazine Group

How to Choose the Right Show

With multiple events competing for your exhibition budget in February, selection criteria become critical. Evaluate each show based on attendee demographics, industry focus, geographic reach, and the competitive landscape. The best show for your company is not always the largest; it is the one where your target buyers are most concentrated.
