How Much Does It Cost to Exhibit at MAGIC 2026?

ShowFloorTips Editorial February 11, 2026

MAGIC is the fashion industry's largest and most influential trade show, operated by Informa Fashion and held twice a year in Las Vegas and New York. MAGIC Las Vegas 2026 (February 17-19) at the Las Vegas Convention Center brought together over 900 exhibitors and tens of thousands of retail buyers to source apparel, footwear, accessories, and lifestyle brands. Whether you are an emerging designer or an established brand, here is what it actually costs to exhibit.

Booth Space Costs

MAGIC booth pricing varies by show section (MAGIC, PROJECT, COTERIE) and booth location:

  • 10x10 inline booth (100 sq ft): $3,800 - $5,200 ($38-$52/sq ft)
  • 10x20 inline booth (200 sq ft): $7,600 - $10,400
  • 20x20 island booth (400 sq ft): $15,200 - $20,800
  • Emerging brand section: $2,500 - $4,500 for smaller configurations

MAGIC Las Vegas and MAGIC New York have different pricing structures. New York pricing at the Javits Center tends to be 15-25% higher than Las Vegas. Contact Informa Fashion's sales team for current rates and availability.

Booth Design and Construction

Fashion trade shows demand visually compelling booth environments that reflect your brand aesthetic:

  • Simple rack-and-display setup: $500 - $2,000
  • Custom 10x10 with branded fixtures: $6,000 - $20,000
  • Custom 20x20 showroom-style: $25,000 - $70,000
  • Rental booth: $2,500 - $10,000 for 10x10

Fashion exhibitors typically invest in quality garment racks ($200-$800), mannequins ($100-$500 each), and professional lighting ($300-$1,500) to properly showcase their collections.

Drayage and Material Handling

  • Standard drayage rate: $130 - $180 per CWT
  • Typical 10x10 booth with garments (500-900 lbs): $650 - $1,620
  • Typical 20x20 booth (2,000-3,500 lbs): $2,600 - $6,300

Ship sample collections via FedEx or UPS to your hotel and hand-carry to the booth when possible to avoid drayage fees on garments.

Electrical and Internet

  • Standard electrical (500W): $300 - $650
  • Enhanced lighting power: $500 - $1,000
  • Wi-Fi: $500 - $1,200

Travel and Lodging

  • Hotel (3 nights): $600 - $1,500 per person
  • Flights: $200 - $600 per person
  • Meals: $60 - $120 per person per day
  • Sample shipping (garments): $200 - $800
  • Ground transportation: $100 - $300 per person

For a team of two over three nights, travel costs range from $2,500 to $6,000. February is a moderate period for Las Vegas hotel rates.

Marketing Materials

  • Lookbook printing: $500 - $2,000
  • Line sheets: $200 - $500
  • Pre-show buyer outreach: $500 - $1,500
  • Order management system: $200 - $500
  • Giveaways: $300 - $1,500

Emerging fashion brands can exhibit at MAGIC for $12,000 - $18,000 with a modest booth and lean team. Established brands with standard 10x10 booths should budget $15,000 - $30,000. Major fashion companies with 20x20 island booths typically spend $60,000 - $120,000+.

Tips to Reduce Your MAGIC Costs

  1. Exhibit at the emerging brand section: Lower booth fees and a targeted buyer audience.
  2. Ship samples to your hotel: Hand-carry garments to avoid drayage on lightweight items.
  3. Use simple, elegant fixtures: Quality garment racks and good lighting can be more effective than an expensive custom booth.
  4. Share a booth: Partner with a complementary brand to split costs.
  5. Pre-schedule buyer appointments: MAGIC's digital platform helps you connect with buyers before the show.
