Arab Health 2026 Exhibitor FAQ: Everything You Need to Know

ShowFloorTips Editorial February 11, 2026

Arab Health (now World Health Expo Dubai) is the largest healthcare exhibition in the Middle East and one of the top 3 globally, attracting 60,000+ attendees and 4,000+ exhibitors to Dubai Exhibition Centre in Dubai, UAE. Whether you are a first-time exhibitor or a returning company, this FAQ covers the most common questions about exhibiting at Arab Health (now World Health Expo Dubai).

How much does booth space cost?

Booth space costs approximately AED 1,800-2,500 per sqm (~$50-68/sq ft) per square foot. A 10x10 booth runs Approximately $4,500 - $6,200 for 9 sqm shell scheme, a 10x20 costs Approximately $9,000 - $12,400 for 18 sqm, and a 20x20 costs Approximately $18,000 - $24,800 for 36 sqm. Pricing varies by hall and booth type (shell scheme vs. raw space). Many countries offer subsidized national pavilions that significantly reduce costs. Contact your trade promotion agency for pavilion options.

When and where is the show?

Arab Health (now World Health Expo Dubai) takes place February 9-12, 2026 at Dubai Exhibition Centre in Dubai, UAE. The show attracts 60,000+ attendees and 4,000+ exhibitors, making it one of the most important events in its industry.

What is the exhibitor registration deadline?

Arab Health opens exhibitor registration approximately 10-14 months before the show. National pavilion organizers may have earlier deadlines. Contact arabhealthonline.com or +971 4 408 2888.

How do I ship my booth materials and equipment?

Shipping to Dubai requires customs clearance. Use a UAE-based freight forwarder for smooth customs processing. ATA Carnets accepted. Allow 4-8 weeks for sea freight. Dubai's free trade zones and efficient port facilities make import relatively straightforward. The official contractor handles on-site material handling.

What hotels are recommended near the venue?

Dubai offers extensive hotel options from $100-$500/night. Hotels near the Dubai Exhibition Centre are convenient. The Dubai Metro provides easy access from most areas. DWTC negotiates hotel blocks with discounted rates. February is peak tourist season in Dubai, so book 3-6 months early.

How many exhibitor badges are included with my booth?

Exhibitor badge allocation based on booth size. All staff require visa eligibility for UAE entry. Many nationalities receive visa on arrival. Pre-registration required through the exhibitor portal.

What are the setup and teardown schedules?

Move-in begins 2-4 days before the show. Shell scheme booths are pre-built. Custom builds must comply with Dubai Exhibition Centre fire safety and structural regulations. Strict inspection before show opening.

What lead retrieval options are available?

Official lead retrieval available through the event's technology partner. Costs vary by package. Bring your own scanning solution as backup. Note: Middle Eastern business culture values personal relationships, so combine digital capture with personal follow-up. Is there parking at the venue?

Dubai Exhibition Centre has on-site parking. Daily rates AED 20-40 ($5-$11). Dubai Metro and taxis provide alternative access. Rideshare services (Careem, Uber) widely available.

What types of companies exhibit at this show?

Exhibitors include medical devices, hospital equipment, pharmaceuticals, laboratory equipment, healthcare IT, imaging and diagnostics, surgical instruments, dental equipment, rehabilitation products, and digital health solutions. Arab Health is the gateway to the Middle East, Africa, and South Asia healthcare markets.
