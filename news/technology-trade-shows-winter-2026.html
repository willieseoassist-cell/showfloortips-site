Exhibiting Tips for Winter Shows

The winter exhibition season brings unique considerations for Technology exhibitors. Keep these strategies in mind as you plan:

  • Use bold graphics at eye level and above to capture attention from across the hall. Your messaging should be readable from at least 15 feet away.
  • Use modular displays that can be reconfigured for different show sizes and booth dimensions.
  • Invest in a clean, open booth layout that invites attendees inside rather than blocking them with tables or barriers.

Building Your Winter Marketing Campaign

Start your pre-show marketing for Winter Technology events well in advance to maximize booth traffic and meeting quality:

  • Send personalized email invitations to your top prospects at least four weeks before the show.
  • Use social media to build anticipation by sharing sneak peeks of your booth design and planned demonstrations.
  • Offer an incentive for booth visits, such as an exclusive white paper, product sample, or prize drawing.

Networking Opportunities

Winter Technology trade shows provide valuable networking opportunities both on and off the show floor:

  • Follow up with new contacts on LinkedIn within 48 hours of meeting, referencing your specific conversation.
  • Join industry association meetings and committee sessions that take place alongside major trade shows for deeper engagement.
  • Host a small breakfast or cocktail event adjacent to the show to create an exclusive networking opportunity for key prospects.
