Tips for May Trade Shows

Preparing for a May event requires attention to seasonal factors, booking timelines, and industry cycles. Here are key considerations for exhibitors:

  • Incorporate interactive elements such as touchscreens, product demos, or hands-on experiences to increase dwell time.
  • Use modular displays that can be reconfigured for different show sizes and booth dimensions.
  • Consider vertical space. Hanging signs and tall structures increase visibility in crowded venues.

"Show organizers report that 78% of exhibitors plan to maintain or increase booth size year over year."

-- Karen Johansson, Head of Trade Show Analytics, PCMA

How to Choose the Right Show

With multiple events competing for your exhibition budget in May, selection criteria become critical. Evaluate each show based on attendee demographics, industry focus, geographic reach, and the competitive landscape. The best show for your company is not always the largest; it is the one where your target buyers are most concentrated.
