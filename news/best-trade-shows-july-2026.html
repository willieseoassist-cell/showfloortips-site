Tips for July Trade Shows

Preparing for a July event requires attention to seasonal factors, booking timelines, and industry cycles. Here are key considerations for exhibitors:

  • Consider vertical space. Hanging signs and tall structures increase visibility in crowded venues.
  • Ensure your booth tells a story. Visitors should understand what you do within the first three seconds of approaching.
  • Lighting is often underestimated. Use spotlights and accent lighting to draw attention to key products and create visual hierarchy.

How to Choose the Right Show

With multiple events competing for your exhibition budget in July, selection criteria become critical. Evaluate each show based on attendee demographics, industry focus, geographic reach, and the competitive landscape. The best show for your company is not always the largest; it is the one where your target buyers are most concentrated.
