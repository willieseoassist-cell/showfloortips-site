How Much Does It Cost to Exhibit at Natural Products Expo West 2026?

ShowFloorTips Editorial February 11, 2026

Natural Products Expo West is the largest natural, organic, and healthy products trade show in the world. Organized by New Hope Network and held annually at the Anaheim Convention Center in California, Expo West 2026 (March 3-6) attracts over 85,000 attendees and 3,600+ exhibitors. For CPG brands in the natural products space, this is the must-attend show to secure retail distribution. Here is what exhibiting actually costs.

Booth Space Costs

Expo West booth pricing depends on your booth size, location, and hall assignment. The Anaheim Convention Center spans multiple halls, with premium pricing for the main North and South halls:

  • Standard booth (8x10 or 10x10, ~80-100 sq ft): $3,200 - $5,500 (~$40-$55/sq ft)
  • 10x20 inline booth (200 sq ft): $8,000 - $11,000
  • 20x20 island booth (400 sq ft): $16,000 - $22,000
  • NEXT Pavilion (emerging brands): $2,500 - $4,500 for smaller display spaces

Booth space at Expo West is extremely competitive. The show typically sells out months in advance, and returning exhibitors get priority rebooking. First-time exhibitors should apply at least 8-12 months ahead and may initially be placed in overflow halls or the Hilton hotel exhibit areas.

Booth Design and Construction

Expo West exhibitors need eye-catching booths that showcase product packaging and allow sampling:

  • Pop-up display with product shelving: $500 - $2,500
  • Custom modular 10x10 with sampling counter: $8,000 - $25,000
  • Custom 20x20 island with refrigeration: $30,000 - $80,000
  • Rental booth (turnkey): $3,000 - $12,000 for 10x10

Many natural products exhibitors invest in high-quality product displays and sampling stations, as tasting is a primary sales driver at Expo West. If your products require refrigeration or special temperature control, factor in $500-$2,000 for equipment rental.

Drayage and Material Handling

  • Standard drayage rate: $120 - $175 per CWT (per 100 lbs)
  • Typical 10x10 booth shipment (600-1,000 lbs): $720 - $1,750
  • Product samples (cases of product): $200 - $800 additional
  • Refrigerated product handling: Additional surcharges apply

Food and beverage exhibitors should account for the weight of product samples. A pallet of product samples can weigh 800-1,500 lbs, significantly increasing drayage costs. Ship samples separately through a freight carrier when possible to avoid show-site drayage premiums.

Electrical and Internet

  • Standard electrical outlet (500W): $300 - $650
  • Refrigeration power (20A dedicated): $600 - $1,200
  • Wi-Fi connection: $500 - $1,500

If you are running refrigerated displays or cooking demonstrations, electrical costs will be higher. Order power early to lock in advance rates.

Travel and Lodging

Anaheim is more affordable than Las Vegas or New York for trade show travel, but rates still spike during Expo West:

  • Hotel (4 nights): $800 - $2,000 per person
  • Flights: $200 - $600 per person (LAX or SNA airports)
  • Meals: $50 - $100 per person per day
  • Rental car or rideshare: $150 - $350 total per person

For a team of three over four nights, travel costs typically run $4,000 - $10,000. The Anaheim Marriott and Hilton adjacent to the convention center book out early but offer walkable convenience.

Product Sampling Costs

This is a cost category unique to food, beverage, and supplement shows:

  • Product samples (3,000-10,000 units): $1,000 - $5,000
  • Sampling cups, plates, utensils: $100 - $400
  • Health department permits: $50 - $200
  • Refrigeration rental: $500 - $2,000

Marketing Materials

  • Sell sheets and printed materials: $300 - $1,000
  • Pre-show email outreach: $500 - $1,500
  • Branded swag: $500 - $2,000
  • New Hope Network advertising: $2,000 - $10,000

Emerging brands in the NEXT Pavilion can get into Expo West for $10,000 - $15,000 total. A standard 10x10 exhibitor should budget $15,000 - $30,000. Large established brands with island booths spend $80,000 - $150,000+.

Tips to Reduce Your Expo West Costs

  1. Apply for the NEXT Pavilion: Smaller, more affordable spaces designed for emerging brands.
  2. Rent your booth: Turnkey rentals save 40-60% over custom builds.
  3. Produce samples in-house: Ship product directly instead of contracting a co-packer for show-specific runs.
  4. Book Anaheim hotels early: Properties within walking distance fill up 4-6 months before the show.
  5. Ship freight directly: Bypass show drayage by hand-carrying lightweight items.
