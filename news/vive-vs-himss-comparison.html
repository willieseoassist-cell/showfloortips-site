VIVE vs HIMSS: Which Should You Exhibit At?

ShowFloorTips · February 9, 2026

Healthcare IT exhibitors face a strategic fork every winter: VIVE in February or HIMSS in March. One is an intimate executive gathering. The other is the industry's massive annual convention. They share a market but serve it in fundamentally different ways. Choose wrong, and you burn budget reaching the wrong audience. Here is what you need to know.

VIVE 2026: The Profile

VIVE 2026 takes place at the Los Angeles Convention Center, February 22-25, 2026. The event draws approximately 6,000 attendees and 300+ exhibitors. It is produced by CHIME (College of Healthcare Information Management Executives) and HLTH, positioning it as an executive-level event focused on the business and strategy of healthcare technology.

Booth pricing: $3,000 - $15,000.

VIVE's calling card is audience quality. The attendee list is heavy with hospital CIOs, health system CTOs, digital health founders, and the investors who fund them. The show deliberately caps its size to maintain an executive concentration that larger events cannot match.

HIMSS: The Profile

HIMSS takes place in Las Vegas, March 3-6, 2026. The event draws approximately 45,000 attendees and is the largest health IT conference in the world. The exhibit hall is a sprawling marketplace where every major EHR vendor, cybersecurity firm, telehealth platform, and interoperability startup competes for attention.

Booth pricing: $5,000 - $30,000.

HIMSS casts a wide net. Attendees range from C-suite executives to clinical informaticists, IT directors, government health officials, and students. The programming spans hundreds of sessions across every conceivable health IT topic.

Head-to-Head: What the Numbers Say

Quality vs. Quantity

This is the central tension. VIVE gives you a concentrated pool of senior decision-makers in a setting where meaningful conversation is possible. HIMSS gives you massive scale, brand visibility, and the chance to reach every layer of a health system's buying committee. A single day at VIVE might yield 15 deep conversations with CIOs. A single day at HIMSS might yield 100 badge scans across all levels.

The question is not which number is bigger. The question is which number maps to how your company actually sells. If your sales process depends on executive sponsorship, VIVE's concentration is worth its weight. If you need broad awareness and pipeline volume, HIMSS delivers it.

Stage in Company Lifecycle

Startups and early-stage companies often get more traction at VIVE, where the intimate format lets them punch above their weight and access executives who would walk past their small booth at HIMSS. Established companies with recognized brands benefit more from HIMSS's scale, where their larger presence reinforces market leadership.

Cost Comparison

VIVE's booth costs run $3,000-$15,000 versus HIMSS's $5,000-$30,000. But the cost gap widens beyond the booth. HIMSS in Las Vegas means expensive hotels during peak convention season, a larger booth staff to cover four days of heavy traffic, and higher production costs to compete visually on a massive show floor. VIVE's smaller format in LA allows a leaner team and simpler booth design without looking out of place.

For a startup spending $50,000 all-in on their trade show strategy for the year, VIVE may be the smarter bet. For an enterprise vendor with a $200,000+ events budget, HIMSS is likely the anchor event with VIVE as a complement.

"VIVE is where you have the conversation. HIMSS is where you prove you belong in the conversation. The best health IT companies show up to both."

-- Healthcare IT News

The Verdict

VIVE and HIMSS are not interchangeable. VIVE is a scalpel: precise, executive-focused, and high-conversion for companies that sell to the C-suite. HIMSS is a megaphone: broad, high-volume, and essential for brand visibility across the entire health IT ecosystem. If your average deal size exceeds $500,000 and requires C-suite sign-off, start with VIVE. If you need market awareness and pipeline at every level, start with HIMSS.

Key TakeawayVIVE delivers executive access at a fraction of HIMSS's cost. HIMSS delivers unmatched scale and brand visibility. Early-stage companies and those selling to the C-suite should prioritize VIVE. Established vendors seeking broad market presence should anchor on HIMSS. The one-week gap between the 2026 editions makes doing both operationally feasible for companies with the budget to maximize healthcare IT exposure.
