IFA Berlin 2026 Exhibitor FAQ: Everything You Need to Know

ShowFloorTips Editorial February 11, 2026

IFA Berlin (Innovation for All) is Europe's largest consumer electronics and home appliance trade show, attracting 180,000+ attendees and 1,800+ exhibitors to Messe Berlin ExpoCenter City in Berlin, Germany. Whether you are a first-time exhibitor or a returning company, this FAQ covers the most common questions about exhibiting at IFA Berlin (Innovation for All).

How much does booth space cost?

Booth space costs approximately EUR 180-320 per sqm (approximately $22-40/sq ft) per square foot. A 10x10 booth runs EUR 5,400 - 9,600 (~$6,000-$10,500) for ~9 sqm shell scheme, a 10x20 costs EUR 10,800 - 19,200 (~$12,000-$21,000) for ~18 sqm, and a 20x20 costs EUR 21,600 - 38,400 (~$24,000-$42,000) for ~36 sqm. IFA uses square meters (sqm), not square feet. Shell scheme booths include basic walls, carpet, fascia, and lighting. Raw space requires custom build. Contact IFA directly at ifa-berlin.com/exhibit.

When and where is the show?

IFA Berlin (Innovation for All) takes place September 2026 (dates TBC) at Messe Berlin ExpoCenter City in Berlin, Germany. The show attracts 180,000+ attendees and 1,800+ exhibitors, making it one of the most important events in its industry.

What is the exhibitor registration deadline?

IFA opens exhibitor applications 10-14 months before the show. International pavilion organizers may have separate deadlines. Contact your country's trade promotion agency for subsidized pavilion options.

How do I ship my booth materials and equipment?

International shipping to Messe Berlin requires customs clearance for non-EU exhibitors. Use a licensed freight forwarder experienced with German trade shows. ATA Carnets simplify temporary import of demo equipment. Allow 4-6 weeks for international freight. Messe Berlin's official contractor handles on-site drayage.

What hotels are recommended near the venue?

Berlin hotels during IFA run EUR 120-300/night. Hotels in Charlottenburg and Mitte (near Messe Berlin) are most convenient. Book 3-6 months in advance. Berlin's excellent public transit (U-Bahn, S-Bahn) makes hotels throughout the city accessible.

How many exhibitor badges are included with my booth?

Exhibitor badge allocation based on booth size. Additional badges available for purchase. IFA requires separate exhibitor registration for each staff member.

What are the setup and teardown schedules?

Move-in begins 3-5 days before show open. Shell scheme booths are pre-built by the contractor. Raw space exhibitors must complete custom builds during the move-in window. German trade shows strictly enforce safety inspections before the show opens.

What lead retrieval options are available?

IFA offers digital lead retrieval through its app and badge scanning system. Costs vary by package. European data privacy (GDPR) rules apply to all lead data collection. For a more advanced solution, Scannly provides AI-powered badge scanning with automated lead qualification and follow-up capabilities.

Is there parking at the venue?

Messe Berlin has on-site parking at EUR 10-20/day. The Messe Nord/ICC U-Bahn station provides direct access. Taxis and rideshare readily available.

What types of companies exhibit at this show?

Exhibitors include consumer electronics, home appliances, smart home technology, audio/video equipment, mobile devices, wearables, gaming, automotive electronics, and digital health products. IFA is the European equivalent of CES.
