COSMEX vs Plastasia: Which Should You Exhibit At?

ShowFloorTips · February 01, 2026

The difference between a forgettable booth and a lead-generating machine comes down to strategy. Choosing between COSMEX and Plastasia is a decision many Business & Trade exhibitors face each year. Both events attract significant audiences and offer strong business development opportunities, but they differ in ways that matter for your budget, target audience, and strategic goals. This detailed comparison breaks down the key factors to help you decide which show deserves your investment.

Show Overview

COSMEX

COSMEX takes place at in Bangkok. Scheduled for November 3-5, 2026, this event is a cornerstone of the Business & Trade exhibition calendar. Booth space is priced at approximately $3,000 - $15,000. The show draws exhibitors and attendees from across the Business & Trade sector, providing concentrated access to buyers, partners, and industry decision-makers in a focused environment designed for business.

Plastasia

Plastasia is held at in New Delhi, with dates set for May 20-23, 2027. Booth pricing is approximately $3,000 - $15,000. This event has established itself as a significant platform for Business & Trade professionals, offering a curated mix of exhibition space, educational programming, and networking opportunities that attract a dedicated audience of qualified buyers and influencers.

Audience and Attendee Profile

The audience composition at each show should heavily influence your decision. COSMEX in Bangkok tends to attract attendees with strong regional representation from the surrounding market, along with national and international visitors drawn by the event's reputation and programming.

Plastasia in New Delhi draws from a different geographic base, which may be more or less aligned with your target market depending on where your ideal customers are located. Consider which event's attendee demographics more closely match your buyer persona, including job titles, company sizes, and purchasing timelines.

Request attendee audit data from both show organizers before making your decision. Key metrics to compare include total attendance, percentage of attendees with purchasing authority, industry segment breakdown, and geographic origin of visitors. The top 20% of exhibitors at any given show capture 80% of the total leads generated.

Cost Comparison

Direct booth space costs tell only part of the story. A comprehensive cost comparison must account for the total investment required to exhibit at each show.

COSMEX estimated costs: Booth space at $3,000 - $15,000, plus design, shipping to Bangkok, team travel and accommodations, marketing materials, and lead capture tools. Bangkok hotel rates, dining costs, and local transportation should be factored into the total budget.

Plastasia estimated costs: Booth space at $3,000 - $15,000, plus similar supporting costs for exhibiting in New Delhi. Depending on your team's location, travel costs may vary significantly between the two destinations, which can swing the total investment by thousands of dollars.

Calculate the cost per qualified lead for each event based on historical data or industry benchmarks. The show with the lower cost per lead, not the lower booth rental, is typically the better investment.

"Custom-built booths generate 35% more leads than rental or modular solutions on average."

-- Karen Johansson, Head of Trade Show Analytics, PCMA

Location and Logistics

Bangkok: As a trade show destination, Bangkok offers its own set of advantages and challenges. Consider flight availability from your team's home base, hotel proximity to the venue, ground transportation options, and the overall cost of doing business in the city. Client entertainment options and dining quality also factor into the experience you can provide to prospects and partners.

New Delhi: New Delhi presents a different logistical profile. Evaluate airport connectivity, venue accessibility, local infrastructure, and the ease of shipping exhibit materials to and from the location. For international exhibitors, factor in visa requirements, customs processes, and time zone considerations for each destination.

The Verdict

Both COSMEX and Plastasia offer legitimate value for Business & Trade exhibitors, but the right choice depends on your specific objectives, target audience, and budget constraints.

Budget-conscious exhibitors should compare the total cost of participation at each event, not just booth rental rates. Factor in travel, accommodations, shipping, and opportunity cost when making your decision.

Many companies with sufficient budget choose to exhibit at both shows, using each event to reach different segments of their target market. If you can only choose one, prioritize the event where your ideal customers are most concentrated.

Key Takeaway Trade show booth visitors spend an average of 5-15 minutes at each exhibit they visit.

Making Your Decision

Request exhibitor prospectuses from both show organizers and compare the data side by side. Speak with companies that have exhibited at both events to get firsthand perspectives on lead quality, attendee engagement, and overall ROI.
